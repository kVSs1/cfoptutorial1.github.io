Beginner CFOP

Beginner CFOP is half intuitive. It has 16 algorithms. It has four parts: Beginner Cross, Beginner F2L, 2-Look OLL (can be 3-Look), and 2-Look PLL. 2-Look OLL and PLL means that from any case of OLL/PLL, you will need to do 2 algorithms in an order one after the other in order to orient or permutate the last layer.
