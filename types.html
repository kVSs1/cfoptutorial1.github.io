Types of CFOP

There are two types of CFOP: Beginner CFOP and Advanced CFOP. The main difference between the two is the number of algorithms (algs). An algorithm is a set of moves/turns done to achieve something. In Beginner Method, there are less algorithms so that it is easier to learn.

There are total 12 algorithms in Beginner CFOP, 9 for OLL and 3 for PLL. The Cross and F2L are intuitive, which means they do not require any algorithms. 12 Algorithms seem small if you compare it to the 78 Algorithms for Advanced CFOP (not including the extra 42 algorithms for most efficient F2L solving)! There are 57 OLL Algorithms and 21 PLL Algorithms in Advanced CFOP.
