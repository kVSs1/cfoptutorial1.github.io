Beginner Cross

The Cross is the first stage of CFOP method. In Beginner CFOP, the Cross is done in away that is easy to understand. Many people first learn to make a Beginner Cross when they start learning how to solve a Rubik's Cube.

The Beginner Cross has two steps, first the daisy, then aligning the edges and bringing them down. For Beginner CFOP, we will be starting with the white side. However, you can use any side for the cross.

Daisy

Daisy is the first step of the Beginner Cross. It can be ommitted but many people say that you should learn it when you are first starting to learn how to solve a rubik's cube. In this step, you get all the edges on the top face. To do it, first find all the white edges. Bring them to the top of the cube. You shall hold the cube such that the white center is on the bottom and the yellow center is on top. This is done to ensure better understanding. You just need to bring all white edges facing up on the yellow side.

Aligning the Edges

After completing the daisy, bring all the edges one by one to the white side. You can hold the cube such that the white side is facing up.
