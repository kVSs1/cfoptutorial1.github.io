Beginner Cross

The Cross is the first stage of CFOP method. In Beginner CFOP, the Cross is done in away that is easy to understand. Many people first learn to make a Beginner Cross when they start learning how to solve a Rubik's Cube.

The Beginner Cross has two steps, first the daisy, then the cross itself. For Beginner CFOP, we will be starting with the white side. However, you can use any side for the cross.
